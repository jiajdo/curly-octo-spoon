Excellent No-Peel Egg Salad!

Photo credit: NYT Cooking

I make this egg salad when I don't feel like cooking (read: all the time) and want a quick, healthy meal.
I even have a life hack for skipping the worst part of making egg saladâ€”peeling boiled eggs (boo! hiss!).

Feel free to adjust the proportions to your taste!

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 0.5 tbsp dijon mustard
  • 1 tbsp sweet relish
  • 0.5 tbsp minced onion

First Step: Bake the Eggs

(Yes, I said bake)

  1. Generously oil a loaf pan
  2. Crack the eggs in the loaf pan
  3. Place loaf pan into a casserole dish and fill dish with water up to the level of the yolks
  4. Cook eggs at 350F for about 40-45 minutes or until yolks are set
  5. After eggs cool, flip eggs onto chopping board and chop finely. No peeling!

Credit for this hack goes to brunchwithbabs. Make sure to follow her on Instagram for more life hacks.

Second Step: Assemble

  1. Combine eggs and the rest of the ingredients into a bowl and mash
  2. Spread mixture on bread or crackers
